How Does a WhatsApp QR Code Actually Work?

FreeQR encodes a standard WhatsApp deep link — https://wa.me/[number]?text=[message] — directly into a QR code. When someone scans it with any modern smartphone camera, the device recognises the URL, opens the WhatsApp app (or WhatsApp Web on desktop), and launches the conversation with your number already populated and the pre-filled message ready to send.

The person scanning does not need to save your number to their contacts first. This dramatically reduces friction and increases the conversion rate from scan to message — which is why WhatsApp QR codes are popular with businesses that rely on inbound enquiries.

  • Works on iPhone and Android with the native camera app — no QR scanner needed
  • Compatible with both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business accounts
  • No server involved — the link is encoded directly in the QR pattern
  • The code never expires as long as the phone number remains active on WhatsApp
